Basic Info

Identity V is an asymmetrical game, which means the two teams have different number of characters. In this game, specifically, it is 1v4 where there are one player that plays the Hunter, and the other four players play the Survivors. The Survivors' goal is to escape, and they can do so by decoding cipher machines. The Hunter, who has different powerful abilities, will try to track and eliminate the Survivors before they escape.
